Segregation

Segregation means sorting waste into different groups to keep the environment clean and make recycling easier.

Waste Type Description Examples
Biodegradable Materials that naturally break down over time Food scraps, leaves, paper
Non-biodegradable Materials that do not decompose and stays in the environment for a long time Plastic, glass, metal
Recyclable Materials that can be repurposed or turned into something new Bottles, cans, cardboard
