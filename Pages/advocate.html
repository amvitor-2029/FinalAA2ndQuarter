Advocate
Advocating for reduced plastic use raises awareness and inspires others to make eco-friendly choices. By speaking up, sharing ideas, and supporting sustainable alternatives, we can drive real change for the planet.
You can:
  1. Carry reusable bags and bottles
  2. Use reusable containers instead of disposables
  3. Choose biodegradable products
  4. Say no to single-use plastics
  5. Join clean-up events in your community

  4. Advocate for plastic-free policies
