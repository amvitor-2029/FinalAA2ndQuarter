The 3 R’s

The 3 R’s—Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle—help lower waste and protect the environment. Reducing cuts waste, reusing gives items another life, and recycling turns old materials into something new.

1
Reduce

Using less waste by choosing only what you need, helping lower the amount of trash produced.

2
Recycle

Sorting materials like paper, plastic, and metal so they can be processed and turned into new products.

3
Reuse

Using items again instead of throwing them away, giving them a longer life and reducing waste.
